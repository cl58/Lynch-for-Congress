Christopher Lynch is the candidate you need!

In a time of a divided Congress and an unwillingness by both Democrats and Republicans to compromise, we need a new candidate who will work for the people. Christopher Lynch is dedicated to serving you, the constituent, and not Washington political elites, Super PACs, or large corporations. Christopher will vote as necessary to get things done in Washington, no matter what. Not unwilling to compromise, he will work together with his colleagues on both sides of the aisle as well as with experts and you, the citizens of New Jersey's 12th district to find the best solutions to the difficult problems Washington is facing. If Christopher is elected as your representative you can rest assured that your best interests will always be the most important thing in his decision making, you will always be able to get in touch to voice your concerns, and you will have a Representative in Congress who wants to get things done.

Christopher Lynch is a native resident of NJ-12, growing up in North Brunswick. A graduate of Montclair State University right here in New Jersey, Christopher has a life-long commitment to the state, so you can always be sure New Jersey is his top priority.
